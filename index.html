About

Yuki Tanaka

Film photographer based in Tokyo, Japan. My work explores the quiet moments between movement — the light that lingers after the crowd has gone, the textures that speak louder than noise.

Each print is made to order on heavyweight museum-grade paper using archival pigment inks, and ships from my studio in Tokyo within 5–7 business days.

For commissions and inquiries: hello@yukitanaka.com
